OpenMagnifier KEYBOARD BINDINGS

TOGGLE MODE
Space, 0 (numerical keypad),/ (numerical keypad): Toggle mode (between Move / Focus / Border / Filter)

MOVE MODE
+, PageUp, 1 (numerical keypad),+ (numerical keypad): Zoom In
-, PageDown, 3 (numerical keypad),- (numerical keypad): Zoom Out
r, 5 (numerical keypad): Reset Zoom and Center
h, 4 (numerical keypad): Left
l, 6 (numerical keypad): Right
k, 8 (numerical keypad): Up
j, 2 (numerical keypad): Down
Enter, Enter (numerical keypad): Goto left edge

FOCUS (Move Mode)
7 (numerical keypad): Focus -
9 (numerical keypad): Focus +

BORDER (Border Mode)

FILTER
4 (numerical keypad): previous filter
6 (numerical keypad): next filter
5 (numerical keypad): no filter

FULLSCREEN
f, / (numerical keypad): Fullscreen

QUIT
Esc, q : QUIT
